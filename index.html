About Us

NavUday Educational Trust of Assam (NETA) is committed to nurturing academic excellence, reasoning skills, and future-ready thinkers.

We believe in building thinkers, not just toppers.

Vision & Aims

To create an inclusive and empowering educational ecosystem that identifies and nurtures academic talent across Assam, enabling students to achieve excellence regardless of their socio-economic background.

Identification

Identifying and encouraging raw academic potential across all districts, from urban centers to the most remote rural areas.

Educational Equity

Ensuring that financial constraints never become a barrier to quality education and academic growth.

Regional Development

Contributing to Assam’s human resource development by nurturing ethical, skilled, and knowledgeable individuals.

Competitive Readiness

Preparing students for competitive examinations by improving thinking speed, accuracy, and problem-solving skills.

Holistic Learning

Encouraging students to think beyond textbooks and build strong analytical and conceptual understanding.

Skill Enhancement

Enhancing time management, question interpretation, and exam temperament.

About the Examination

NavUday Educational Trust of Assam conducts a State Level Merit Scholarship Examination for students of Class V to Class IX. The examination provides students with a competitive platform to assess their academic potential at the state level.

Medium of Examination:

  • English
  • Assamese
  • Bengali
  • Bodo

Subjects Covered:

  • English Grammar
  • Science
  • Mathematics
  • Reasoning / Mental Ability
  • General Knowledge
